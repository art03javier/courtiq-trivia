How To Play NBA Trivia

Welcome to NBA Trivia! Follow these instructions to enjoy the game:

  1. Getting Started: Choose a difficulty level by clicking on one of the options (Simple, Intermediate, Advanced, and Expert).
  2. Taking the Trivia: Once you've selected a difficulty level, click the "Take Trivia" button to start the game.
  3. Answering Questions: You will be presented with a series of multiple-choice questions. Click on the option you think is correct.
  4. Verifying Answers: After selecting an answer, click the "Verify Answer" button to check if you're correct.
  5. Next Trivia: Once you've verified the answer, click the "Next Trivia" button to move to the next question.
  6. Scoring: Your score will be displayed at the end of the trivia. Each correct answer earns you a point.
  7. Return to Trivia: If you want to go back to the trivia after reading instructions, click the "Back to Trivia" button.
  8. Scroll to Top: At any point, you can scroll back to the top of the page by clicking the "Scroll to Top" button (labeled as "Top"). This button is always available and is particularly useful after navigating through different sections.
  9. Return to Level Selection: If you are in the "Score Achieved" section and want to return to the difficulty level selection page, click the trophy icon button (labeled as "level") at the bottom.
